<p><p>The Electrically Heated Glass market includes Laminated Glass and Hollow Glass types. Laminated Glass consists of multiple layers bonded together, often with a heating element embedded, providing safety, insulation, and uniform heat distribution. It is widely used in automotive and architectural applications. Hollow Glass, or insulated glass, features two or more panes separated by a gap, often filled with inert gas, and integrates heating elements for energy-efficient thermal control. Both types enhance comfort, reduce condensation, and improve energy efficiency in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
