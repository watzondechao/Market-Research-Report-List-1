<p><p>The aprotic solvents market includes dipolar aprotic solvents and inert solvents. Dipolar aprotic solvents, such as dimethyl sulfoxide and N,N-dimethylformamide, are characterized by their strong polarity, enabling them to dissolve a wide range of polar and ionic compounds, making them essential in chemical synthesis and reactions. Inert solvents, on the other hand, are non-polar solvents that do not react with solutes or undergo chemical changes; they serve primarily as mediums for chemical processes without participating in reactions.</p></p>
