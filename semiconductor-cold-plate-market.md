Single-stage modules utilize a single refrigeration cycle to achieve cooling, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate cooling efficiency. In contrast, multistage modules incorporate multiple cooling stages, allowing for greater thermal management and efficiency, ideal for high-performance applications needing significant temperature reductions. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, each requiring effective thermal management solutions.</p></p>
