<p><ul><li>Open Wedge Sockets</li><li>Closed Wedge Sockets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wedge sockets market comprises two main types: open wedge sockets and closed wedge sockets. Open wedge sockets feature a design that allows easy access for inserting or removing fasteners, making them ideal for applications requiring frequent adjustments. In contrast, closed wedge sockets provide enhanced security by enclosing the fastener, minimizing the risk of loosening during operation, which is preferable in high-vibration environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and machinery, each with specific operational requirements.</p></p>
