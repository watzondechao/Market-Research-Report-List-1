<p><p>The high-end coffee machine market primarily consists of two types: drip coffee machines and capsule coffee machines. Drip coffee machines brew coffee by dripping hot water over ground coffee, allowing for large quantities and customizable flavors, appealing to traditional coffee enthusiasts. In contrast, capsule coffee machines offer convenience and consistency, using pre-packaged coffee pods for quick brewing. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with drip machines favoring craftsmanship and capsule machines prioritizing ease of use and minimal cleanup.</p></p>
