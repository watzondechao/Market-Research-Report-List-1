<p><p>The LED lighting supply market features two main types: constant voltage and constant current. Constant voltage systems deliver a fixed output voltage, making them ideal for applications like LED strips that require uniform brightness over longer distances. In contrast, constant current systems maintain a consistent current output, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of LEDs, particularly in high-power applications. Each type addresses specific needs, with constant voltage favored for versatility and constant current preferred for precision in brightness and efficiency.</p></p>
