<p><ul><li>PCM Dedicated Line Access</li><li>DDN Dedicated Line Access</li><li>Fiber Access</li><li>SDH</li><li>ADSL Dedicated Line Access</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dedicated Internet Access (DIA) services encompass various market types that cater to specific connectivity needs. PCM Dedicated Line Access offers reliable, circuit-switched connections for businesses requiring stability. DDN Dedicated Line Access provides dedicated digital lines for reliable data transmission. Fiber Access leverages high-speed fiber optics for superior bandwidth and performance. SDH (Synchronous Digital Hierarchy) offers advanced multiplexing capabilities for efficient data handling. ADSL Dedicated Line Access combines DSL technology with dedicated bandwidth, serving businesses with flexible needs for speed and reliability.</p></p>
