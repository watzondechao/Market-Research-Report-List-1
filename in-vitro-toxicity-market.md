<p><p>The In Vitro Toxicity Market is categorized into two primary types: Dose Response and Threshold Response. Dose Response studies assess how varying levels of a toxic substance correlate with the extent of biological effect, providing insight into risk assessment and regulatory compliance. In contrast, Threshold Response focuses on determining the minimum concentration required to elicit a toxic response, indicating a safe exposure level. Together, these approaches enhance the understanding of chemical safety and guide product development in various industries.</p></p>
