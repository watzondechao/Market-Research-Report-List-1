<p><p>Feedthrough markets consist of three main types: electrical, mechanical, and fluid feedthroughs. Electrical feedthroughs facilitate the passage of electrical signals or power between different environments, crucial for electronics and telecommunication sectors. Mechanical feedthroughs enable the transfer of motion or mechanical forces, commonly used in robotics and industrial automation. Fluid feedthroughs allow the transfer of liquids or gases, essential in various applications including hydraulic systems and chemical processing. Together, these feedthrough types support diverse industries by ensuring reliable connections in challenging environments.</p></p>
