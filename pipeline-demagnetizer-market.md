<p><ul><li>Permanent-magnet Demagnetizer</li><li>Stored-energy Demagnetizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipeline demagnetizer market consists of two main types: permanent-magnet demagnetizers and stored-energy demagnetizers. Permanent-magnet demagnetizers utilize strong permanent magnets to neutralize magnetic fields, effectively preventing magnetic contamination in pipelines. In contrast, stored-energy demagnetizers rely on electrical energy storage mechanisms to generate strong magnetic pulses, which disrupt unwanted magnetic fields. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity of pipeline systems and preventing operational issues caused by magnetism, especially in industries like oil and gas.</p></p>
