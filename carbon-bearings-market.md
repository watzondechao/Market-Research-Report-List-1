<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Plain Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon bearings market can be classified into two main types: rolling bearings and plain bearings. Rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to reduce friction between moving parts, making them suitable for high-speed applications. They are known for efficiency and durability. Plain bearings, on the other hand, consist of surfaces sliding against each other without rolling elements, offering simplicity and reliability in low-speed applications. Both types benefit from carbon materials, promoting enhanced wear resistance, reduced friction, and improved overall performance.</p></p>
