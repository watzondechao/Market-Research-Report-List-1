<p><p>The PET Preform Injection Molding System is a specialized technology used for producing preforms, which are the intermediate products shaped like test tubes and are essential for manufacturing PET bottles and containers. The system involves the injection of molten PET resin into a mold, where it cools and solidifies into preforms that are later expanded and shaped into bottles through blow molding.</p><p>The PET Preform Injection Molding System Market is experiencing notable growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable and recyclable packaging solutions in various industries, particularly in the food and beverage sector.
