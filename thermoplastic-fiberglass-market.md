<p><p>The Thermoplastic Fiberglass market includes various types, primarily Spun Glass Fiber and Drawn Glass Fiber. Spun Glass Fiber is produced by spinning molten glass, resulting in fine, lightweight fibers with applications in insulation and composite materials. Drawn Glass Fiber, on the other hand, involves drawing molten glass through fine orifices to create stronger, more durable fibers, commonly used in high-performance composites. Both types serve critical roles in diverse industries, enhancing product performance and structural integrity.</p></p>
