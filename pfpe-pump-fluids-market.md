<p><p>PFPE pump fluids are specialized perfluoropolyether lubricants used in various applications, classified into four main types: D-type, K-type, Y-type, and Z-type. D-type fluids offer excellent thermal stability and low volatility, suitable for high-temperature environments. K-type fluids are designed for better viscosity characteristics, ideal for low-temperature operations. Y-type fluids provide enhanced lubrication in high-speed applications, while Z-type fluids focus on reducing friction and wear, making them suitable for extreme conditions. Each type caters to specific operational requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
