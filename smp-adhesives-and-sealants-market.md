<p><p>The SMP (Silyl Modified Polymer) adhesives and sealants market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component SMP adhesives are ready to use and cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component SMP adhesives require blending a resin and hardener before application, offering enhanced performance for demanding environments. Both types provide excellent adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for construction, automotive, and industrial uses.</p></p>
