<p><p>The breast cancer screening market encompasses several key types, including mammography, breast MRI, and breast ultrasound screenings. Mammography is the most common method, utilizing X-ray technology to detect abnormalities. Breast MRI offers enhanced imaging using magnetic fields and radio waves, ideal for high-risk patients or those with dense breast tissue. Breast ultrasound screening employs sound waves to evaluate breast tissue, often as a supplementary tool. Each method plays a vital role in early detection and improving treatment outcomes for breast cancer.</p></p>
