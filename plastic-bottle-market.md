<p><p>The plastic bottle market includes various types based on material properties. **Polystyrene (PS) bottles** are lightweight and rigid, often used for single-use applications. **Polyethylene (PE) bottles**, available in HDPE and LDPE forms, are durable, chemical-resistant, and widely used for packaging liquids. **Polycarbonate (PC) bottles** are transparent, impact-resistant, and ideal for reusable containers. **Polypropylene (PP) bottles** are heat-resistant and flexible, suitable for hot-fill products. **Other** materials cater to niche applications, offering specialized features like enhanced barrier properties or biodegradability, meeting diverse industry needs.</p></p>
