<p><strong>The Heat Sealing PTFE Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat sealing PTFE fabrics market is categorized into porous and non-porous types. Porous PTFE fabrics feature a permeable structure that allows air and moisture to pass through, making them suitable for applications requiring breathability, such as insulation and protective clothing. Non-porous PTFE, on the other hand, is impermeable, providing excellent barrier properties against liquids and gases, ideal for industrial and medical applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of PTFE, offering durability, chemical resistance, and thermal stability.</p></p>
