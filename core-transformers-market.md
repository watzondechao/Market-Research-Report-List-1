<p><strong>The Core Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core transformers can be categorized into steel laminated cores and solid cores. Steel laminated cores are constructed from thin steel sheets stacked together, which enhances efficiency by reducing energy losses due to eddy currents. They are commonly used in power transformers due to their superior magnetic properties. In contrast, solid cores consist of a single piece of material, offering simpler construction and lower production costs, but may have higher energy losses. Each type serves distinct applications based on efficiency and cost considerations.</p></p>
