<p><p>The home nursing bed market comprises two primary types: manual and electric nursing beds. Manual nursing beds require users or caregivers to adjust the bed's height and position using mechanical levers or hand cranks, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, electric nursing beds utilize a powered system for easy adjustments at the touch of a button, enhancing convenience and comfort for patients. Both types cater to varying needs, with electric beds typically preferred for their advanced features and ease of use.</p></p>
