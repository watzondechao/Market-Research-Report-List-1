<p><p>The automotive collision detection sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: front collision detection sensors and side collision detection sensors. Front collision detection sensors are designed to identify potential impacts ahead of the vehicle, utilizing technologies like radar and cameras to prevent accidents by alerting drivers or activating braking systems. Side collision detection sensors monitor the sides of vehicles to detect impending side impacts, enhancing safety during lane changes or intersection navigation, ultimately contributing to overall vehicle safety and accident prevention.</p></p>
