<p><p>The Logic Analyzer market is primarily divided into two segments: Logic State Analyzers (LSA) and Logic Timing Analyzers. LSA focus on capturing and analyzing digital signals to debug and validate circuit designs by monitoring multiple digital lines simultaneously. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal performance in digital electronics and embedded systems development.</p></p>
