<p><p>A Ring Laser Gyroscope (RLG) is a highly precise inertial navigation device that measures angular rotation based on the Sagnac effect. <p><p>The Ring Laser Gyroscope (RLG) market comprises two primary types: Single-Axis and Three-Axis gyroscopes. Single-Axis gyroscopes measure rotation around one axis, making them suitable for simple applications where limited movement tracking is required. In contrast, Three-Axis gyroscopes track rotation across three perpendicular axes, providing comprehensive orientation data essential for advanced navigation systems in aerospace, automotive, and robotics. The increasing demand for precise motion sensing drives the adoption of these technologies across various industries, enhancing overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
