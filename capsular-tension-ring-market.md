<p><p>A Capsular Tension Ring (CTR) is a medical device used in cataract surgeries to maintain the shape of the capsular bag, which holds the lens of the eye. This device is particularly beneficial in cases where the capsular bag is compromised or weak, providing structural support and improving surgical outcomes. Its primary function is to facilitate the insertion of intraocular lenses (IOLs) and to help mitigate post-operative complications, such as lens dislocation.</p><p>The Capsular Tension Ring Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of cataracts, the aging population, and advancements in surgical techniques.
