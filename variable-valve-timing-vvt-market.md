<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) is an advanced engine technology that optimizes the timing of the valve openings and closings to improve performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions. The market for VVT is divided into two types: Continuous VVT and Non-continuous VVT. Continuous VVT allows for smooth, real-time adjustments to valve timing, enhancing engine responsiveness across various conditions. In contrast, Non-continuous VVT operates in predefined steps, offering less flexibility but typically at a lower cost, making it suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
