<p><ul><li>Gear Type</li><li>Encoded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary limit switch market comprises two primary types: gear type and encoded type. Gear type switches utilize mechanical gears to detect rotational positions, providing reliable and straightforward operation in various industrial applications. Encoded type switches, on the other hand, use sensors to interpret the rotational position electronically, offering higher precision and flexibility for advanced automation systems. Both types are essential for enhancing control and safety in machinery, but they cater to different operational needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
