<p><p>The non-magnetic capacitor market encompasses various types, including Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) and Copper Clad Capacitors. MLCCs are compact, offering high capacitance values and stability, making them ideal for consumer electronics and automotive applications. Copper Clad Capacitors, on the other hand, feature a copper layer that enhances conductivity and reduces electromagnetic interference. Both types cater to diverse industries by providing reliable performance in environments where magnetic interference must be minimized, supporting advancements in technology and electronics design.</p></p>
