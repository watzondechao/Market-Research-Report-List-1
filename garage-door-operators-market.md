<p><ul><li>Chain Driven Garage Door Openers</li><li>Screw Driven Garage Door Openers</li><li>Belt Driven Garage Door Openers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The garage door operators market primarily includes three types: chain-driven, screw-driven, and belt-driven openers. Chain-driven openers use a metal chain to lift the door, offering strength and affordability, making them popular for heavy doors. Screw-driven openers utilize a threaded rod mechanism, requiring less maintenance and delivering moderate speed and noise levels. Belt-driven openers, known for their quiet operation, use a rubber belt, making them ideal for residential settings where noise reduction is a priority. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
