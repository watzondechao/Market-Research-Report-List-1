<p><p>Food retail display cases come in two primary market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical display cases utilize height, providing ample storage while allowing customers to view products at eye level. They are often used for beverages and packaged items. Horizontal display cases, on the other hand, spread products across a wider surface area, making them ideal for bakery items, meats, and cheeses, enhancing accessibility and visibility. Each type caters to different product presentations and retail environments to optimize customer engagement.</p></p>
