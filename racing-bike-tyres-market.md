<p><p>The racing bike tyre market comprises three main types: clincher tyres, which use a separate inner tube and are easy to install and repair; tubular tyres, which are sewn around an inner tube and offer superior performance but require more skill to install; and tubeless tyres, which eliminate the inner tube, reducing the risk of punctures and allowing for lower air pressure for improved traction. Each type caters to different riding styles and preferences, affecting performance and handling.</p></p>
