<p><p>The bus door system market encompasses two primary types: electrical actuator systems and pneumatic actuator systems. Electrical actuator systems utilize electric motors to open and close bus doors, offering precise control and energy efficiency. In contrast, pneumatic actuator systems rely on compressed air to operate doors, providing robust performance and quicker response times. Both systems are designed to enhance passenger safety and accessibility while accommodating diverse operational environments and preferences in highly populated urban transit systems.</p></p>
