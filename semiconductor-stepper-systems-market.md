<p><p>The semiconductor stepper systems market primarily consists of two key types: stepper motor systems and drive systems. Stepper motor systems utilize precise rotation control for tasks like wafer alignment and photolithography in semiconductor manufacturing. Drive systems complement these motors by providing the necessary power and control signals, ensuring accurate motor movement. Together, these systems enable high precision and efficiency in fabrication processes, crucial for producing advanced semiconductor devices with tight tolerances. Their synergy is vital for optimizing production capabilities in the industry.</p></p>
