<p><p>Laser Communications Terminals (LCTs) are integral to advanced satellite communication systems, categorized into Low Earth Orbit (LEO) and Geosynchronous Orbit (GEO) markets. LEO LCTs facilitate high-speed data transmission with minimal latency, making them ideal for applications like Earth observation and internet services. In contrast, GEO LCTs operate at higher altitudes, providing broader coverage and stable connections, typically suited for more traditional communication services. Both markets leverage laser technology to enhance bandwidth and signal quality, driving advancements in global communication networks.</p></p>
