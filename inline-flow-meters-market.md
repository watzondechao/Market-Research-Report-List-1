<p><p>The inline flow meters market consists of two primary types: digital flow meters and mechanical flow meters. Digital flow meters utilize advanced electronic components and sensors to provide accurate, real-time measurements of fluid flow, often featuring the ability to connect to data systems for monitoring. In contrast, mechanical flow meters rely on physical moving parts, such as gears or turbines, to measure flow, making them generally simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types serve various industries, offering unique benefits depending on specific application needs.</p></p>
