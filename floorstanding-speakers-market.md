<p><p>Floorstanding speakers are categorized into two main types: active and passive speakers. Active speakers are self-powered, incorporating built-in amplifiers, which allows them to connect directly to audio sources without needing external amplification. This makes setup simpler and more convenient. In contrast, passive speakers require an external amplifier to power them, offering flexibility in terms of sound customization and integration with different audio systems. Both types are popular in home theater setups and music listening environments, catering to varying preferences.</p></p>
