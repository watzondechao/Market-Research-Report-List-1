<p><p>The Polymer Waterproof and Breathable Material market encompasses various types based on the polymer used. PE (Polyethylene) waterproof breathable membranes are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for basic applications. PP (Polypropylene) membranes offer enhanced durability and chemical resistance, ideal for more demanding environments. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) membranes provide excellent elasticity and flexibility, commonly used in high-performance gear. e-PTFE (expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene) membranes feature superior breathability and waterproof properties, making them ideal for advanced outdoor and protective clothing. Each type caters to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
