<p><p>The direct-to-consumer genetic testing market encompasses three main types. Diagnostic screening involves tests to identify genetic disorders or predispositions to diseases, empowering individuals with crucial health information. Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) allows prospective parents to screen embryos for genetic conditions before implantation during in vitro fertilization, ensuring healthier pregnancies. Relationship testing determines biological relationships, such as paternity or ancestry, enabling individuals to explore familial connections and heritage. Each segment caters to distinct consumer needs in the increasingly personalized health landscape.</p></p>
