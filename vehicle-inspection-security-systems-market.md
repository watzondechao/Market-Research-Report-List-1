<p><p>Vehicle Inspection Security Systems encompass three primary types: permanent, fixed, and portable. Permanent systems are embedded in the road, offering a seamless integration that allows for continuous monitoring without obstructing traffic flow. Fixed systems are attached to the road's surface and provide stationary inspection points, often equipped with cameras and sensors for real-time analysis. Portable systems are mobile, enabling inspection capabilities at various locations, making them adaptable for temporary deployments during events or in high-traffic areas where immediate security assessments are needed.</p></p>
