<p><p>The baby wipe warmer market features two primary heating types: top-down heating and bottom-up heating. Top-down heating warms wipes from the top down, allowing for consistent warmth, but may lead to uneven temperatures. In contrast, bottom-up heating gently heats the wipes from the bottom, ensuring more even warmth throughout the stack. Each type caters to different user preferences for convenience and efficiency, influencing purchasing decisions in the growing market for baby care products.</p></p>
