<p><p>The benchtop power supply market features two primary types: single output and multi-output power supplies. Single output types deliver a single voltage and current, ideal for simple applications or individual circuits. In contrast, multi-output types provide multiple voltages and currents simultaneously, catering to more complex projects requiring various power levels. The choice between these types depends on user needs; single outputs offer focused simplicity, while multi-outputs enhance versatility for diverse testing and development scenarios.</p></p>
