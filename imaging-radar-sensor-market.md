<p><strong>The Imaging Radar Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Range Radar (SSR)</li><li>Mid-Range Radar and Long-Range Radar (MRR/LRR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The imaging radar sensor market is categorized into three main types based on range: Short-Range Radar (SSR), Mid-Range Radar (MRR), and Long-Range Radar (LRR). SSR is typically used for applications like parking assistance and collision avoidance in vehicles, providing high-resolution images over short distances. MRR balances range and resolution, suitable for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). LRR, capable of detecting objects at great distances, is ideal for applications in surveillance, automotive safety, and autonomous navigation systems.</p></p>
