<p><strong>The Hyperspectral Infrared Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR)</li><li>Short Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long wave infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hyperspectral infrared cameras are categorized into four types based on wavelength ranges. Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) cameras capture short wavelengths for applications like agriculture and environmental monitoring. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) extends the range, useful for detecting moisture and chemical analysis. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) cameras operate in a range suited for thermal imaging and defense applications. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) cameras specialize in detecting heat signatures, making them essential for surveillance, firefighting, and building diagnostics. Each type serves specific industrial and research needs.</p></p>
