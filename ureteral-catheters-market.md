<p><strong>The Ureteral Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermittent Cathater</li><li>Folley Cathater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ureteral catheters market primarily consists of two types: intermittent catheters and Foley catheters. Intermittent catheters are designed for single-use to drain urine from the bladder at specific intervals, promoting patient autonomy and reducing the risk of infections. In contrast, Foley catheters are indwelling devices that remain in place for longer durations, allowing continuous urine drainage. Both types serve critical roles in urology, catering to patients with various conditions requiring urinary management and support in clinical settings.</p></p>
