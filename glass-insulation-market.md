<p><p>The glass insulation market comprises several key types, including Insulating Glass Units (IGUs), glass wool, and cellular glass. IGUs are multi-pane assemblies designed for enhanced thermal performance, commonly used in windows. Glass wool is a fibrous material that provides excellent thermal and acoustic insulation, often utilized in both residential and commercial applications. Cellular glass, made from crushed glass, offers high resistance to heat and moisture, making it suitable for industrial insulation and specialty use in construction.</p></p>
