<p><p>The soundproof glass market encompasses three main types: hollow glass, vacuum glass, and laminated glass. Hollow glass features air-filled spaces to reduce noise transmission, while vacuum glass employs sealed airless space to provide superior insulation and soundproofing. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers of glass with an interlayer that absorbs sound vibrations, enhancing acoustic performance. Each type offers distinct benefits tailored to specific noise reduction needs, making them suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
