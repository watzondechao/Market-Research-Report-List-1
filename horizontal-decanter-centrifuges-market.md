<p><p>Horizontal decanter centrifuges are crucial in separation processes, classified mainly into two types: two-phase and three-phase decanter centrifuges. A two-phase decanter separates solid from liquid, commonly used in wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, a three-phase decanter separates two immiscible liquids along with solids, making it ideal for applications like oil-water separation and biomass processing. Both types enhance efficiency in various industries by enabling effective separation and clarifying materials, leading to improved product quality and operational efficiency.</p></p>
