<p><strong>The Shoulder Orthoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoulder orthoses market is divided into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide movement and support, allowing for controlled range of motion while aiding recovery and rehabilitation. They are often used post-injury or surgery to enhance functional mobility. In contrast, static orthotics immobilize the shoulder to promote healing by restricting movement, ideal for conditions like fractures or tendonitis. Both types serve specific therapeutic purposes, tailored to patient needs for recovery and improved shoulder function.</p></p>
