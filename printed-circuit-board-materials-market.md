<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) materials market encompasses various types, including photoresist, copper-clad laminate (CCL), glass fabric, and others. Photoresist materials are crucial for transferring circuit designs onto substrates during manufacturing. CCL serves as a foundational layer, combining copper with insulating materials for conductivity. Glass fabric provides structural integrity and thermal stability to PCBs. Other materials may include prepregs, insulating layers, and various coatings, each contributing to the performance and durability of printed circuit boards in electronic applications.</p></p>
