<p><p>The Indirect Pilot Solenoid Valve market includes primarily two types: Normally Closed (NC) and Normally Open (NO) valves. Normally Closed valves remain shut in their default state and open when energized, making them suitable for applications requiring safety and control over fluid flow. Conversely, Normally Open valves allow fluid to flow until they are energized, making them ideal for systems that need immediate action or pressure regulation. Both types are critical in automation and fluid control applications across various industries.</p></p>
