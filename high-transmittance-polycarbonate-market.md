<p><ul><li>Phosgene Interface Method</li><li>Melt Transesterification Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Transmittance Polycarbonate market comprises various production methods, notably the Phosgene Interface Method and the Melt Transesterification Method. The Phosgene Interface Method involves polycarbonate synthesis through the reaction of bisphenol A and phosgene, yielding high clarity and durability. In contrast, the Melt Transesterification Method utilizes a transesterification process between bisphenol A and dimethyl carbonate, offering an environmentally friendly alternative. Both methods are essential for producing polycarbonate with superior optical properties and applicability in industries like electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
