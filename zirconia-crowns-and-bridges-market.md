<p><p>Zirconia crowns and bridges are popular dental restorations made from zirconium dioxide, known for their strength and aesthetic appeal. The market is primarily divided into two types: layered zirconia and monolithic zirconia. Layered zirconia crowns and bridges feature an outer layer of porcelain for enhanced aesthetics while retaining the strength of zirconia underneath. In contrast, monolithic zirconia restorations are crafted from a single block of zirconia, offering high durability, reduced fracture risk, and simpler fabrication, making them an attractive option for many dental applications.</p></p>
