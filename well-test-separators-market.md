<p><p>The well test separators market comprises various types based on the phase separation capabilities. Three-phase test separators can separate gas, oil, and water, providing more detailed analysis for complex reservoirs. Four-phase test separators further enhance the separation process by allowing for the extraction of additional components like solids or specific gas fractions, catering to advanced testing requirements in petroleum engineering.</p></p>
