<p><p>The polypropylene film for capacitors market comprises primarily two types: metallized PP film and BOPP film. Metallized PP film features a thin layer of metal, enhancing electrical properties and capacitance, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film offers superior mechanical strength and thermal stability, widely used in capacitors for energy storage and energy-efficient applications. Both types play essential roles in various electronic devices, contributing to the efficiency and reliability of capacitor performance.</p></p>
