<p><p>Membrane dryers are essential for removing moisture from gases and air through selective permeation. The market comprises two primary types: porous and non-porous membrane dryers. Porous membrane dryers allow vapor to pass through while retaining larger molecules, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, non-porous membrane dryers utilize a dense structure to separate moisture under pressure, ensuring high efficiency in drying processes. Both types are integral for enhancing energy efficiency and maintaining optimal conditions in numerous industries.</p></p>
