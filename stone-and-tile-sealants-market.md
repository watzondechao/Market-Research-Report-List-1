<p><p>The stone and tile sealants market comprises several key types, including epoxy, cementitious, vinyl ester, and others. Epoxy sealants are known for their strong adhesion and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Cementitious sealants offer durability and are typically used in construction settings. Vinyl ester sealants combine the properties of epoxy and polyester, providing excellent resistance to harsh chemicals.
