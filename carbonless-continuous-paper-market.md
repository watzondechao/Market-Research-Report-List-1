<p><p>The carbonless continuous paper market is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer carbonless paper consists of a single sheet treated with special chemicals that allow for image transfer upon pressure. Multi-layer carbonless paper involves multiple layers, with each layer capable of producing a copy when pressure is applied to the top layer. These types are widely used in applications such as invoice printing and invoicing systems, offering efficient and environmentally friendly solutions for duplicating documents without traditional carbon sheets.</p></p>
