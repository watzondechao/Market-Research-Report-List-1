<p><ul><li>Active NOx Adsorbers</li><li>Passive NOx Adsorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NOx Adsorbers market is divided into two main types: Active and Passive NOx Adsorbers. Active NOx Adsorbers use chemical reactions and controlled conditions to actively capture and reduce nitrogen oxides from exhaust gases, typically incorporating a regeneration process. In contrast, Passive NOx Adsorbers rely on adsorption and desorption processes without the need for external energy, capturing NOx during low-temperature conditions and releasing it for reduction in more favorable settings. Each type serves distinct applications in emissions control.</p></p>
