<p><p>The Transcranial Magnetic Stimulator (TMS) market is categorized into several types based on stimulation methods. sTMS (single-pulse TMS) involves delivering isolated pulses for research and therapeutic purposes. pTMS (paired-pulse TMS) evaluates cortical excitability by applying paired stimuli. rTMS (repetitive TMS) uses rhythmic pulses to modulate neural activity, often for treating depression and anxiety. nTMS (navigated TMS) employs imaging techniques to improve accuracy in targeting brain areas, enhancing research and clinical outcomes. Each type serves specific therapeutic and research needs.</p></p>
