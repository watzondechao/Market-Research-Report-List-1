EL-20 has approximately 20 moles, offering moderate emulsifying properties for various formulations. EL-30, with around 30 moles, provides stronger emulsification and stabilization, ideal for more demanding applications. "Others" include variations that may cater to specialized industrial or cosmetic uses.</p></p>
