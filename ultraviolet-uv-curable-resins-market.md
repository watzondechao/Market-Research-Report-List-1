<p><strong>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne UV Resins</li><li>100% Solids UV Resins</li><li>Waterborne UV Resins</li><li>Powder UV Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Curable Resins market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties. Solventborne UV Resins contain solvents and offer flexibility in application but involve volatile organic compounds. 100% Solids UV Resins are robust, containing no solvents, ideal for creating durable coatings. Waterborne UV Resins are eco-friendly, using water as a carrier, reducing VOC emissions. Lastly, Powder UV Resins are free of solvents and can be cured by UV light, providing efficient, waste-reducing options in coatings and printing applications.</p></p>
