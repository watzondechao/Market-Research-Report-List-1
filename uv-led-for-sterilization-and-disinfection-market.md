<p><strong>The UV LED for Sterilization and Disinfection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED sterilization and disinfection market comprises three main types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. </p><p>UV-A LEDs emit long wavelengths and are primarily used for curing applications but have limited germicidal effects. UV-B LEDs, with medium wavelengths, can cause some microbial damage but are less effective than UV-C. </p><p>UV-C LEDs, emitting short wavelengths, are highly effective for sterilization, targeting DNA and RNA to eliminate bacteria and viruses, making them ideal for healthcare, water treatment, and air sanitation applications.</p></p>
