<p><p>The industrial cleaning chemicals market comprises various types, including hydrotropes and D-limonene. Hydrotropes are surfactants that enhance the solubility of cleaning agents in water, making them effective for formulations where solubility is a challenge. D-limonene, derived from citrus fruits, is a biodegradable solvent known for its grease-cutting properties and pleasant aroma, commonly used in eco-friendly cleaning products. Both play crucial roles in improving cleaning efficiency and are integral to sustainable cleaning solutions in various industries.</p></p>
