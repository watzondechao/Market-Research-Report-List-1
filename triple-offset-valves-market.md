<p><p>Triple offset valves are key components in fluid control systems, available in various types based on their actuation methods. Manual type valves require physical operation for opening and closing, offering simplicity and reliability. Electric type valves utilize electronic actuators for precise control, making them suitable for automated systems. Pneumatic type valves operate through compressed air, providing fast and efficient actuation. Hydraulic type valves use hydraulic pressure for operation, ideal for high-pressure applications, each serving distinct needs in industrial processes.</p></p>
