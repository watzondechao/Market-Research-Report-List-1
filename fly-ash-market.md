<p><strong>The Fly Ash Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F</li><li>Class C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fly ash is categorized mainly into two types: Class F and Class C, based on their chemical composition and reactive properties. Class F fly ash originates from burning anthracite or bituminous coal and contains lower calcium content, making it ideal for high-strength concrete applications. In contrast, Class C fly ash comes from lignite or sub-bituminous coal, rich in calcium, which can enhance early strength and provide self-cementing properties. Both types are utilized in construction for durability and sustainability.</p></p>
