<p><p>The Powder Metallurgy High-Speed Steel market is primarily categorized into two types: Molybdenum Type and Wolfram Type. Molybdenum Type high-speed steel is known for its excellent hardness and wear resistance, making it suitable for cutting tools and industrial applications. In contrast, Wolfram Type is characterized by higher tungsten content, providing enhanced heat resistance and toughness, ideal for high-performance tooling. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, each requiring specific performance attributes.</p></p>
