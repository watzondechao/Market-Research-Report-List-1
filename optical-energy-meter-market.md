<p><p>The optical energy meter market is segmented into three main types: pyroelectric, photodiode, and others. Pyroelectric energy meters detect changes in temperature due to incoming radiation, making them suitable for measuring high-energy pulses. Photodiode meters utilize semiconductor devices to convert light into electrical current, offering high sensitivity for continuous measurements. Other types may include advanced technologies like silicon carbide sensors or thermal detectors. These diverse options cater to various applications, from scientific research to industrial processes, enhancing measurement accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
