<p><p>The voltage translators market includes three main types: unidirectional voltage translators, bidirectional voltage translators, and direction-controlled voltage translators. Unidirectional translators facilitate voltage level shifting in one direction, ensuring compatibility between devices operating at different voltages. Bidirectional translators allow communication between two devices, enabling voltage level adjustments in both directions. Direction-controlled voltage translators offer flexibility by allowing users to set the transmission direction, enhancing versatility in complex circuits and minimizing the need for additional components.</p></p>
