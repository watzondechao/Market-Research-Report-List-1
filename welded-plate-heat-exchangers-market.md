<p><strong>The Welded Plate Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-welded</li><li>Fully-welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welded plate heat exchangers market is primarily divided into two types: semi-welded and fully-welded heat exchangers. Semi-welded units feature a combination of welded and removable plates, allowing for easier cleaning and maintenance while offering robust thermal performance. In contrast, fully-welded heat exchangers consist of entirely welded plates, ensuring superior strength and resistance to high pressures and extreme temperatures. Both types are used in various industries, including chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing, to enhance thermal efficiency and optimize heat transfer.</p></p>
