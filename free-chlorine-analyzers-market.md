<p><p>The Free Chlorine Analyzers market includes various technologies, such as X-ray Fluorescence and UV Fluorescence. X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) analyzes chlorine levels by measuring the fluorescence emitted when X-rays interact with chlorine atoms, providing precise elemental detection. UV Fluorescence, on the other hand, relies on the absorption of ultraviolet light by chlorine, producing a fluorescent response that indicates concentration levels. Both methods are essential for water quality monitoring, enabling accurate assessment and management of chlorine levels in various applications.</p></p>
